The goal of this project was to merge the fast-paced nature of Blinkit’s delivery with a high-energy, comic-book aesthetic. By utilizing a vertical "Webtoon" style layout, the ad targets a mobile-first audience, turning a mundane household mishap into a dramatic, relatable narrative. The bold outlines and vibrant colors were chosen to grab attention within the first few seconds of a social media scroll.
